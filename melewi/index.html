We're a kickass design studio with a global perspective

Every day at Melewi is spent working with startups, Fortune 500 companies, and everyone in-between to turn their wildest ideas into an exceptional experience their customers don't want to put down.

Collaborating with clients located around the world just makes sense to us. We live and work from Singapore, Melbourne, Manila, Berlin, Copenhagen, Paris and anywhere a plane can take us.

Our view is global: we travel to get inspired, gain perspective, and build products and businesses that speak to everyone, everywhere.

WeInvest
FINTECH WEBSITE, WEBAPP, MOBILE APP

WeInvest is a fintech comparison platform that helps users explore, compare and select their best investments opportunities. We worked closely with the team to create their entire webapp from the ground up. With complex systems and regulation, as well as a heavy amount of data and text, we worked tirelessly to design and iterate a product that was seamless and easy to use.

- Asia Pacific
- Product Strategy, UX, UI, Web, Mobile, Marketing

MyKicks
SPORTSTECH FOOTBALL TRAINING MOBILE APP

As Founder Institute's top 2016 startup, myKicks was on a mission to revolutionise football training with their uber-cool augmented reality, A.I. powered app. We worked with them through multiple user-validated pivots and iterations to craft an intuitive user experience and visual direction that footballers worldwide would love.

- Asia Pacific, Europe & USA
- Product Strategy, UX, UI, Mobile
