Hello! My name is Melissa Ng,
also known as @thedesignnomad

I'm a product & design strategist, and the founder of remote design agency Melewi. I also help teams and businesses around the world find success with location independence.

I became a digital nomad by accident, discovering I could work from anywhere in the world 8 years ago while designing an app on a boat in rural Vietnam for a client in Sydney.

Since then, I've founded Melewi, a location-independent Product, UX & UI Design studio working with businesses from around the world - from startups in Australia, San Francisco and Singapore, to global companies like Visa, McDonalds and Samsung, we've designed experiences for millions of users in over 48 cities across 6 continents.

I spend my mornings running Melewi and mentoring product teams across multiple timezones, and my afternoons chasing down adventures (this year it's all about skiing!).

I believe life is too short to spend boxed up in just one place, and I've done some of my best work on boats in Cambodia, on beaches in the Maldives, and from the top of mountains in New Zealand.
