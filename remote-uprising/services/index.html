WHAT WE COVER
Expertise across every aspect of remote teams
Communication planning
Communication breakdown is fatal for distributed teams. We make up for decreased visibility with well-planned communication policies and robust tech solutions for everyone involved.
Team Culture Setup & Growth
A great company culture is difficult to achieve and maintain — especially for remote teams. Using the values framework and systems we’ve created, we’ll build you a united and purpose-driven team culture and working environment.
Operations, Policies & Systems
We’ll help you create a comprehensive set of operational systems and remote working policies customised to your business, and that will keep your workers and managers aligned, and stand the test of a location-independent set up.
Hiring, Onboarding & Firing
Having a great team means hiring the right people with the right values, who work well together. From attracting and vetting the best candidates, to the foolproof ways to onboard them, we guide you through getting, retaining and managing the best remote workers for your team.
Employee ROI & Productivity
Productivity is a top concern for managers of remote teams. We help you keep track of your employee’s productivity and growth with the right tools, metrics and goal-setting.
Leading & Managing Remotely
Remote leadership and management can often prove tricky. We help you gain awareness of the common issues that arise, and equip you with the right knowledge and strategies to successfully lead from afar.
