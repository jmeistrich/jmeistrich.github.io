Our story.
Our decade of remote business experience makes us the best to help you with yours.
With 10 years of practical knowledge and unprecedented expertise, Remote Uprising is brought to you by Melissa Ng and the Melewi team.

Today, Melewi is a million-dollar, fully distributed, dedicated team in 6 countries, working with clients across continents — from game-changing startups to titans of the Fortune 100 and 500.

We’ve shared our expertise across Asia-Pacific and Europe with businesses like Dropbox and Salesforce. And we’ve had our story told on the likes of Forbes, The Straits Times, Channel News Asia and Les Echos.

check out melewi.net

With our track record comes an arsenal of hard-earned experience and strategic solutions on remote working. Remote Uprising is about bringing that knowledge to other businesses, and elevating the rapidly growing movement of distributed teams around the world.

For us, the remote movement has become our everyday. And we’re incredibly excited to bring this revolution to you.
