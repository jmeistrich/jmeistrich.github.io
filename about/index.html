About Me

Past

I studied at Carnegie Mellon University where I spent most of my time making games in the Game Creation Society and interned on Boom Blox at EA Games. Then I worked on the Sensors and Location platform in Windows 7 at Microsoft. Then I prototyped experiences for devices of the future and helped start the Surface tablet project in Microsoft's Applied Sciences Group.

Now

I'm building a product to make organization easy and awesome while traveling the world with a backpack and a camera. I'm trying to be a very productive startup founder while traveling and living a different lifestyle every three months. It's going well so far.

I built the website for Impending, Inc. It features an animated sprite for the logo, parallax scrolling, twinkling stars, animated moving clouds with parallax for a 3D effect, and a dynamically scaling background based on the size of the content.

Mayhem

Intunesity
iOS game
Abstract musical painting toy

Experience synesthesia! Paint colors around the screen to compose a pretty song. Each of three colors corresponds to a different instrument. Choose from twelve different instruments to make music out of your paintings.

Picopy
website
Copy photos from Picasa/Google+ to Facebook

Picopy is a tool for copying photos between web services. It runs entirely in client-side javascript and is designed to make it easy to plug in new services. It currently supports copying from Google+/Picasa to Facebook.
